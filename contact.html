What is a sponsor?

Brothers in Arms" is a heartfelt initiative to honor the bravery and camaraderie of Indian and Canadian soldiers who stood side by side during World War I. Their unwavering spirit and sacrifices made a significant impact on the course of history. This memorial park aims to preserve their legacy, inspire future generations, and serve as a lasting tribute to their shared commitment to freedom and peace. Join us in creating a space where their stories of courage and unity will live on forever.
