What is a Donor?

A donor is a vital part of the Brothers in Arms Memorial, helping to preserve the legacy of Indian and Canadian soldiers who stood together in World War I. Your contribution supports the creation of this meaningful space, ensuring their courage and sacrifice are never forgotten. By donating, you become a part of this tribute, fostering remembrance, unity, and education for future generations. Join us in honoring history—every donation builds a legacy.
