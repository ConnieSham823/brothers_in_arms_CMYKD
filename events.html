108 Trees Memorial – Honoring Indian Soldiers - May 26, 2018

On May 26th, 2018, the first phase of an inspiring memorial project was successfully completed in partnership with the City of London and Trees for 3 Dots. The project saw the planting of 108 trees along the Veterans Memorial Parkway in London, Ontario. This site, less than a 2-minute drive from the 401 Freeway, serves as the location for the memorial. The trees were planted in rows and columns, much like soldiers standing together in formation, symbolizing a "Living Guard of Honour" for the Indian soldiers who fought alongside the Canadian forces during World War I...
