Letters from Soldiers

A Wounded Sikh Soldier to His Father (January 18, 1915)

"Tell my mother not to go wandering madly because her son, my brother, is dead. To be born and to die is God's order. Some day we must die, sooner or later. If I die here, who will remember me? It is a fine thing to die far from home."
